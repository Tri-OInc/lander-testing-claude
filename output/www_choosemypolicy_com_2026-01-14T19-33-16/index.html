Why Final Expense Insurance Matters
Social Security only pays a one-time death benefit of $255* — but today, the average funeral costs between $7,000 and $12,000. Final Expense Insurance provides your family with a tax-free cash benefit to help cover burial, cremation, unpaid bills, and more. No medical exam is required, and many applicants between 50-85 may qualify — even if they've been turned down before.
Common Questions Answered
Do I need to take a medical exam?
No. Most plans don't require any medical exam or health questions - making it easy to get covered.
What can the benefit be used for?
Anything: funeral costs, cremation, medical bills, or other personal expenses. The payout goes directly to your chosen beneficiary.
Guaranteed Issue Plans
You don't need perfect health. Acceptance is guaranteed and coverage won't be canceled for age or condition.
